Basic Vocal Processing Engine

I took a 6 bar melody from a record I am working on and sent it through a BiQuadFilter node, Convolver node, and Gain node. To successfully initiate this chain, I had to understand the process of connecting the output of one node to the input of another node. The Convolver node is not adjustable and follows the BiQuadFilter node, while preceding the Master Gain Knob.
